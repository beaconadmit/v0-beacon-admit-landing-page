## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Migration failures in prod | Medium | High | Test on staging first, backup before apply |
| Data loss during schema change | Low | High | Use additive migrations only, no DROP COLUMN |
| Rate limiting blocks legitimate traffic | Low | Medium | Start with generous limits, monitor |
| Supabase connection limits | Medium | Medium | Configure pooler, monitor connections |
